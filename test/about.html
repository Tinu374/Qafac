About Us

Qafac is a leading petroleum company based in Qatar. We are committed to providing sustainable energy solutions to our customers while prioritizing safety, environmental responsibility, and community engagement. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced team ensure the highest standards of quality and reliability in every aspect of our operations.

Company Overview

Founded in the 2000s, QAFAC is a leading petroleum company based in Qatar. Our mission is to deliver high-quality petroleum products while prioritizing safety, sustainability, and innovation. Since inception, we have remained committed to exceeding the expectations of our customers and stakeholders.

Facilities and Infrastructure

QAFAC operates state-of-the-art facilities across Qatar, equipped with advanced technology and infrastructure. Our facilities include state-of-the-art refineries, storage terminals, and distribution networks, allowing us to efficiently produce, store, and distribute petroleum products to our customers.

Corporate Social Responsibility

At QAFAC, we are committed to social corporate responsibilities and sustainability. We prioritize environmental conservation, community engagement, and ethical business practices. Through initiatives such as Environment Fest 2017-2020 and company programs, we strive to make a positive impact on society and the environment.

Partnership and Collaboration

QAFAC collaborates with industry partners, government agencies, and research institutions to drive innovation and enhance our operations. Our strategic partnerships enable us to leverage expertise, resources, and networks to deliver value to our customers and stakeholders.

Accreditations and Certifications

QAFAC holds various accreditations and certifications, including ISO for quality management. These certifications demonstrate our commitment to delivering high-quality products and services that meet the conditions and requirements of international standards.
